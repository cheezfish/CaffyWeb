WHAT CHANGED AND WHY:
---------------------
1. Fallback prices set BEFORE the fetch
   - Previously: data-price="0" on buttons until the fetch succeeded
   - Now: data-price="5000" (50 AED) immediately, overwritten with real Stripe price if fetch works
   - This means a UAE user who adds to cart while the fetch is hanging still gets the right price

2. AbortController with 6-second timeout
   - Previously: fetch() had no timeout — if the network dropped the request silently, it hung forever
   - Now: after 6 seconds with no response, the request is aborted and the catch block fires
   - 6 seconds is generous enough for slow connections but won't leave users stuck on "Loading..."

3. clearTimeout(priceTimeout) on success
   - Cancels the abort timer cleanly if the fetch comes back before 6 seconds

NOTE: The fallback price (50.00 AED / 5000) is hardcoded here. If you ever change your Stripe
price, update these fallback values too. They are purely a safety net.
