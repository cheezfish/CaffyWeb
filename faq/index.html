Product Information

What is CAFFY?

CAFFY is a revolutionary energy supplement designed to enhance focus, memory, and energy levels using a scientifically-backed blend of nootropics.

How should I use CAFFY?

Take CAFFY pouches on a full stomach for optimal results. Place the pouch between your gum and cheek for 20-30 minutes. Consume up to three pouches per day.

What are the ingredients?

CAFFY contains a proprietary blend of Guarana, Taurine, Alpha-GPC, Rhodiola Rosea, and L-theanine. These, as well as some flavouring and stabilising compounds, are dropped into a food safe fibrous pouch.

What makes CAFFY different from other energy products?

CAFFY stands apart by delivering a clean, sustained energy boost without the crash associated with traditional energy drinks. Our scientifically formulated blend of nootropics works to enhance cognitive function while providing energy, creating a balanced effect that improves focus and mental clarity without jitters or anxiety.

How much caffeine is in each CAFFY pouch?

Each CAFFY pouch contains approximately 80mg of caffeine derived naturally from guarana. This is roughly equivalent to a cup of coffee, but with an extended release formula that provides sustained energy over a longer period.

How long does the effect last?

Most users experience effects for 2-3 hours after use. The precise duration may vary based on individual metabolism, tolerance to caffeine, and other factors.

Is CAFFY suitable for vegetarians/vegans?

Yes, CAFFY pouches are entirely plant-based and suitable for both vegetarians and vegans. Our products contain no animal-derived ingredients.

Are CAFFY pouches sugar-free?

Yes, CAFFY pouches are sugar-free. We use flavoring systems that don't impact blood sugar levels, making them suitable for those monitoring their sugar intake.

Usage & Best Practices

Can I use CAFFY while exercising?

Yes, many of our customers use CAFFY before or during workouts. The combination of caffeine and nootropics can help improve workout intensity and mental focus during exercise.

Can I use CAFFY with other caffeine products?

We recommend being mindful of your total caffeine intake. If you're consuming CAFFY alongside other caffeine sources like coffee or tea, consider reducing your overall intake to avoid exceeding recommended daily caffeine limits (400mg for most adults).

Is CAFFY safe for daily use?

Yes, CAFFY is formulated to be safe for daily use when consumed as directed. We recommend not exceeding three pouches per day and taking occasional breaks from caffeine consumption.

Can I use CAFFY if I'm sensitive to caffeine?

If you're sensitive to caffeine, we recommend starting with half a pouch and assessing your tolerance. The L-theanine in our formula helps moderate the effects of caffeine, which may make it more suitable for those with caffeine sensitivity compared to other caffeine products.

When is the best time to use CAFFY?

CAFFY is most effective when used during periods requiring increased focus and energy. Many users prefer taking it in the morning or early afternoon. We don't recommend using CAFFY within 4-6 hours of bedtime as it may affect sleep quality.

Are there any side effects?

When used as directed, CAFFY typically doesn't cause adverse effects. However, as with any caffeine product, some individuals may experience mild side effects such as increased heart rate or difficulty sleeping if used too close to bedtime. We recommend starting with one pouch to assess your tolerance.

Health & Safety

Is CAFFY safe during pregnancy or breastfeeding?

As with most caffeine products, we recommend consulting with your healthcare provider before using CAFFY if you are pregnant, breastfeeding, or planning to become pregnant.

Are there any medical conditions that should avoid CAFFY?

Individuals with certain medical conditions such as heart problems, high blood pressure, or caffeine sensitivity should consult with a healthcare professional before using CAFFY. Always seek medical advice if you're taking prescription medications that might interact with caffeine or other ingredients.

Is CAFFY FDA approved?

CAFFY complies with all UAE food safety regulations. Our ingredients are generally recognized as safe (GRAS) and our manufacturing facilities adhere to strict quality control protocols.

Does CAFFY contain any allergens?

CAFFY pouches do not contain common allergens such as nuts, dairy, soy, or gluten. However, if you have specific allergies, please review our complete ingredient list or contact our customer service team.
